5. Features / Highlights
Business Problem
Zomato needs to identify why certain regions are underperforming and address a significant gap between customer acquisition and customer churn. Without a centralized view, it is difficult to see which age groups or cities are driving the most value.

6.Goal of the Dashboard
Track Revenue: Monitor total sales amount (987M) and order volume (150K).

Analyze Retention: Visualize the "Gain vs. Lost" customer ratio to improve loyalty programs.

Geographic Analysis: Identify top-performing cities like Tirupati and Bikaner to scale successful local strategies.

Demographic Targeting: Pinpoint the core user age group (20‚Äì30 years) for targeted advertising.

- Walkthrough of Key Visuals
Main Dashboard (Sales Overview): * Sales by Year: A line chart showing a peak in 2018 (0.41bn) followed by a downward trend, signaling a need for strategic intervention.

Category Analysis: Comparative cards for Non-Veg, Veg, and Other food types, showing Non-Veg as the dominant revenue driver (148K ratings).

Top Sales City: A bar chart identifying Tirupati and Electronic City as the highest revenue generators.

User Performance Insights:

Churn Tracking: High-impact visuals showing 33K Lost Customers vs. 12K Gained Customers, segmented by gender.

City Performance Table:

A granular breakdown of Orders, Gain/Lost Users, and Sales per city (e.g., Abids & Koti, Hyderabad) to assist local operations managers.

7. Business Impact & Insights
Churn Alert: The dashboard reveals that churn (33K) is nearly 3x higher than acquisition (12K). This insight suggests a shift in focus from "New Customer Acquisition" to "Retention and Loyalty."

Prime Demographic: Since the 20‚Äì25 age bracket is the most active, promotional campaigns should be focused on platforms like Instagram and YouTube.

Regional Strategy: Bikaner shows the highest number of Active Users and Ratings, suggesting it is a "High Engagement" hub compared to Tirupati, which is a "High Value" hub.
